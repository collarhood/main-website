Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering custom merchandise

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity is typically 30 pieces. However, we can accommodate smaller orders for certain products. Contact us with your requirements for a custom quote.

How long does production and delivery take?

Standard production time is 7-14 days after design approval. Delivery takes an additional 3-5 days depending on your location. Rush orders can be accommodated - contact us for express delivery options.

Do you provide design services?

Yes! Our team offers free design consultation. If you have your own design, great! If not, our designers will help create the perfect design for your team based on your vision and preferences.

What printing methods do you use?

We offer multiple printing techniques including screen printing, dtf print, embroidery, and sublimation. We'll recommend the best method based on your design, fabric, and budget.

Can I see a sample before bulk production?

Absolutely! We provide digital mockups for approval before production. Physical samples can be arranged for large orders (500+ pieces) at an additional cost.

Do you ship across India?

Yes! We deliver to all major cities and towns across India including Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata, Pune, Jaipur, and more. Track your order from our facility to your doorstep.

What fabric quality do you use?

We use premium 280-350 GSM fabric for hoodies and sweatshirts, ensuring maximum comfort and durability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI and bank transfer. For bulk orders, we offer flexible payment terms with 50% advance and 50% on dispatch.
