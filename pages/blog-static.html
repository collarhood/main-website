Understanding GSM: Choosing the Right Fabric Weight

GSM (grams per square meter) determines the thickness and quality of your merchandise. Learn which GSM is perfect for different products and seasons.

Jan 28, 2026 • 6 min read
Read More

GSM (Grams per Square Meter) is the metric that defines fabric weight and thickness. Understanding GSM helps you choose the perfect merchandise for your needs.

Common GSM Ranges:

  • 160-180 GSM: Light t-shirts, perfect for summer and daily wear
  • 180-220 GSM: Medium weight tees and polos, versatile for all seasons
  • 280-350 GSM: Premium hoodies and sweatshirts, maximum comfort and durability
  • 160-200 GSM (Micro PP): Sports jerseys, breathable and performance-focused

At Collarhood, we use 280-350 GSM for our hoodies because it strikes the perfect balance between comfort, durability, and premium feel. Your crew deserves the best!

Pro Tip: Higher GSM doesn't always mean better. Choose based on your use case - sports teams need breathable fabrics while college fests prefer cozy hoodies.

Printing Guide

Printing Techniques: Puff, Screen, and Embroidery Explained

Understanding different printing methods helps you choose the perfect technique for your design. Each method has unique advantages and best use cases.

Jan 20, 2026 • 7 min read
Read More

1. Puff Print

Creates a raised, 3D effect that adds dimension to your design. Perfect for bold logos and text. Extremely popular for streetwear and college merchandise.

2. Screen Printing

Traditional method that's cost-effective for bulk orders. Vibrant colors that last through multiple washes. Ideal for large quantities with simple designs.

3. Embroidery

Premium stitched designs that exude quality. Perfect for polo shirts, caps, and corporate merchandise. Extremely durable and sophisticated.

4. DTG (Direct to Garment)

Digital printing method great for complex designs with multiple colors. Best for small batches and photo-realistic prints.

Our Recommendation: Puff print for streetwear hoodies, embroidery for caps and polos, screen print for bulk t-shirts. Mix and match based on your design!

Complete Guide to Planning College Fest Merchandise

From budget planning to delivery timelines, everything you need to know to successfully order merchandise for your college fest or event.

Dec 15, 2025 • 8 min read

Timeline Planning

  • 8-10 weeks before: Start design discussions and get quotes
  • 6-8 weeks before: Finalize design and place order
  • 4-6 weeks before: Production time (varies by quantity)
  • 2-3 weeks before: Quality check and delivery
  • 1 week buffer: Always keep time for unexpected issues

Budget Breakdown

  • 50-60% on core apparel (hoodies, t-shirts)
  • 20-25% on accessories (caps, bags)
  • 15-20% on printing and customization
  • 5-10% buffer for last-minute additions

Pro Tip: Order 10-15% extra quantities in popular sizes (M, L, XL). They sell out fast!

Color Psychology in Merchandise: Choosing the Right Palette

Colors evoke emotions and define your brand. Learn how to choose the perfect color palette that resonates with your crew's identity and values.

Dec 1, 2025 • 6 min read
Read More

Popular Color Associations:

  • Yellow/Gold: Energy, optimism, creativity - perfect for fest merch
  • Black: Sophistication, power, timeless - works with everything
  • Blue: Trust, professionalism, calm - great for corporate teams
  • Red: Passion, energy, boldness - ideal for sports teams
  • Green: Growth, nature, balance - eco-focused groups
  • Purple: Creativity, luxury, wisdom - cultural societies

Combination Tips:

  • Use 2-3 main colors max to avoid clutter
  • High contrast makes designs pop (black + yellow, white + navy)
  • Consider how colors look on different fabric bases
  • Test colors on both light and dark backgrounds
