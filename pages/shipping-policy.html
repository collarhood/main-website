SHIPPING POLICY

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

1. Shipping Coverage

We ship custom merchandise across India:

  • All major cities and metros
  • Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities
  • Remote areas (may have extended delivery times)
  • International shipping available on request for bulk orders

2. Shipping Methods

We partner with reliable courier services:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days (free on orders above ₹5,000)
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days (additional charges apply)
  • Bulk Orders: Custom shipping arranged based on quantity and destination
  • Courier Partners: Delhivery, Blue Dart, FedEx, India Post

3. Shipping Charges

Shipping costs are calculated based on:

  • Order weight and volume
  • Delivery location (metro vs remote areas)
  • Shipping method selected
  • Free shipping on orders above ₹5,000 (standard delivery)
  • Orders below ₹5,000: ₹100-₹250 shipping fee
  • Express shipping: Additional ₹200-₹500

4. Processing Time

Order processing timeline:

  • Production: 7-14 business days for custom orders
  • Quality Check: 1-2 business days
  • Packaging: 1 business day
  • Dispatch: Within 24 hours after packaging
  • Rush Orders: 5-7 business days (available at extra cost)

5. Order Tracking

Stay updated on your order:

  • Tracking number sent via email and SMS once shipped
  • Real-time tracking available through courier website
  • Proactive updates at key delivery milestones
  • Customer support available for tracking queries

6. Delivery Timeline

Estimated delivery times (after dispatch):

  • Metro Cities: 2-3 business days
  • Tier 2 Cities: 3-5 business days
  • Tier 3/Remote Areas: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days (metros only)

7. Delivery Issues

In case of delivery problems:

  • Failed Delivery: Courier will attempt 2-3 times
  • Address Issues: Contact us immediately to update address
  • Damaged Package: Don't accept delivery, contact us immediately
  • Lost Package: We file claim with courier and reship at no cost
  • Delays: We proactively communicate and provide updates

8. Bulk Order Shipping

For bulk orders (50+ units), we offer special shipping arrangements including split deliveries, multiple locations, and custom packaging. Contact our team to discuss logistics for your bulk order.
