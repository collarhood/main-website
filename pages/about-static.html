ABOUT COLLARHOOD

We're on a mission to bring identity to life through premium custom merchandise

Our Story

Born from the vibrant energy of college fests and the desire for unique team identity, Collarhood was founded by IIT alumni who understood the power of custom merchandise in building community and pride.

We saw countless college clubs, corporate teams, and societies struggling to find quality merchandise that truly represented their spirit. Generic designs, poor quality materials, and unreliable vendors were the norm.

That's when we decided to create Collarhood - India's premier platform for premium custom merchandise. We combine cutting-edge designs with high-quality materials and reliable service to bring your team's vision to life.

With 20+ product types available for customization - from hoodies and jerseys to accessories and promotional items - we're ready to serve college fests, corporate teams, sports clubs, and societies across India. Every piece we create carries the passion and identity of the team it represents.

Our Mission

To empower every team, club, and organization in India with premium quality custom merchandise that celebrates their unique identity and fosters a sense of belonging.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and innovative custom merchandise platform, setting new standards for quality, creativity, and customer experience in the industry.
